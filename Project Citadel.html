Project Citadel

#
Project Citadel was a series of the most advanced arcanotech research and development operations carried out in the later years of the Dulane Empire. These operations were highly secretive, unknown to even the emperor, for the OMR feared sabotage or theft by Xorhosians, The Lich, or other opposition. The primary purpose of Project Citadel was to develop tools and weapons to aid the Empire in its various wars. Each operation had a single physical location referred to as its “Citadel” where people from various disciplines worked and lived to experiment and produce arcantotech tools. As such these citadels have facilities for recreation, eating, personal space, hazardous material storage, experiment chambers, fabrication process, ect. The leader of each operation was called the “key”, they typically were the only people that had full knowledge of their individual operation.

Each citadel followed a hollistic approach to their task, considering arcanotech machines, rituals and curses, and even derriving systems from magical creatures.
