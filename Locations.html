Tower of Preservation
Mortus, South of Ko's Chain, TODO
A monument during the ending days of the D.E. The tower was a kind of hospice for people who contracted powerful curses for incurable magical illnesses. The tower had three main legs that came together halfway, then spread back out at the same rate to support a large dome (space needlesque).

Gaiga Novosel, Master of locusts, Anointed Knight Commander, Slayer of gyhruig’qe, 3rd to throne, and Key to the Locked Door. Gaiga Novosel was the first key to Operation Locked Door but after an accidental venture into the astral sea, Gaiga contracted a deadly disease that no priest, arcanist, nor druid could heal. So the legendary hero Gaiga went to sleep in a stasis pod in The Tower of Preservation, passing the key to Locked Door to Shayla Anbib. Several other paragons also were stored in the tower, though none as powerful as Gaiga. Gaiga’s affliction was a mental sickness that caused him to have outbursts of madness, which would be temporarily treated by automatic injection from his legendary armor. These injections are quite painful and release a large burst of steam.
