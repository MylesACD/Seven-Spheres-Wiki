Black Gods

The Black Gods also known as the Great Fiends or The Wicked Nine. They are a collection of 3 Archdevils, 4 Demon Lords, Utumno and Ataxia. When things go wrong the Black gods are usually blamed, and one of them or an underling is often responsible.

Although they are referred to by mortals as a group and rarely even a plotting body, these entities do not work together. The Archdevils are orderly beings and work together but only within themselves. The Demon Lords are pretty much in perpetual war with everything all the time, while their goals or enemies may align they do not even consider forming a coalition.
