Origin

Long ago during the Anno Novis a Jotschael named Spytchey, bound Dao into an adamantine flask. After the Uruk-hero Gilgrach killed Spytchey, she went through the three trials required to open the flask and released the dao, in return for their freedom Nahice offered Gilgrach a wish. She wished for the power to destroy Khus Thauk, the Claws of Domination, so that their demonic power would no longer taint Ethos. The strength of Gilgrach's will was so overwhelming that as Nahice forged the gauntlet that would crush one of the accursed blades, the dao was made into the jade giving it their full power and sentience.

Years later when Gilgrach's quest to find one of the Khus Thauk was finnally successful, she gripped the blade in the massive jade gauntlet, closed her fist, and turned the sword dust. But there is no power in Mortus, Jotunhiem, or Hell that can withstand Damn'niz'Khus. And as the blade was annihilated so was Gilgrach, leaving behind a crater nearly a mile wide. All that remained was the Jade Hand, now completely black, smoking at the bottom. The crater became a holy site and a large city, named Crater, grew around it.
