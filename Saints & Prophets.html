Great Prophets

Apaizl - The first prophet, lived through the events Zeta Toll, and was the first group of mortals to ever meet a celestial and first to speak with Warm Light of Dawn. Apaizl took up the mantle of leader for all humanity, but not all mortals as the Uruk were still considered the enemy during that time. One of Apaizl's greatest achievements was the construction of a massive organ that was played by the winds of Orthos, and would serve as the most direct line of communication between mortals and celestials for centuries. Apaizl's organ remains intact, but secretly guarded by ████ █████ █████████ █.

Uruk Nesh - Second prophet, vanquished the first necromancer.
