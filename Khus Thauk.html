Khus Thauk

Origin

In the legendary battle between the two demi-gods: Warm Light of Dawn and Daam’nizkhus demon lord of domination, WLD dealt a critical blow against the demon lord, cutting off his right hand. In the aftermath his numerous massive claws were harvested, these blades would be fashioned into swords of incredible demonic power. Most of these weapons were created by great uruk smiths, others were made by demons seeking to capture a piece of their former lord’s power.

Name: The original name for this set of blades in old-uruk translates to “Claws of the Master”. Khus Thauk modernly can be translated to any combination of Blades/Claws of The Master/Domination.

Blade of Rebirth

When this blade kills a mortal their soul is consumed and used to fuel the victims transformation into a demon under control of the wielder.

Monsoon Blade

When this blade kills something, a swarm of spectral daggers are summoned from Sordidus, finding the wielder's enemies.
