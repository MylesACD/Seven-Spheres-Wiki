Airships

Airships are legally classifeid by the following four aspects: lift, propulsion, hull, weapon systems. Each aspect has a range of technological sophistication which when summed across the different aspects determines the class.

These technological levels can be generally defined as:
    Primitive: found in the natural world(not required to be mundane)
    Magical: Created by spell or enchantment
    Arcanotech: Using arcane machines
Lift: The methodology or resources used to raise the the airship's elevation.
