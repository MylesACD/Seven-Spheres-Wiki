JOST

The Joint Orthosian Security Taskforce was an organization setup principally to monitor individuals that could pose a threat to the stability of society. JOST functions as a collaboration between the Throngdorian Republic and the Dulane Empire, but was expaned to include the Qubean Khanate and --TODO--.
