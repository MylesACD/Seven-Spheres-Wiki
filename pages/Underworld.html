Underworld

The Shadow Fell, also known as the plane of terror or darkness, is a realm shrouded in mystery. The plane was created after the Second Great Seal but before the Eon of Memory. Created by a secret plot between Utumno, Narisa, and Ataxia the plane was made with the consent or knowledge of Quultar. The plane is filled with many foul things including undead, fiends, even crazier undead, Barrakix, and chiefly the God of Shadows. The nature of the Shadow is not known to Quultar, Rico, or Empyreon, it was created through the chaos of imagination. The Underworld holds an especially strong connection to the lower planes, astral sea, and Mat Trang.
