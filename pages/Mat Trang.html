Mat Trang

TODO marc god lore review
The first and larger of the two moons that orbit Ethos, Mat Trang is the resting place for mortal souls. The moon has two halves the light side and dark side as it is old enough to be tidally locked. The dark side is the dwelling of Utumno, a limbo land where souls wander to damnation or to be thrust back to mundane realm for sinister purpose. The bright side of the moon is lorded by Nychta, who protects worthy souls. For this reason, Mat Trang is often called the soul of Quultar because it houses the souls of mortals.

Nychta and the moon are huge parts of ancestor worship across Ethos, in many cultures full moons are nights of remembrance, a time to pay respect. Conversely new moons are considered unlucky or unprotective nights when foul forces cause mayhem.
