Age of Grandeur

"The age when light was stronger than steel,
and our monuments to the gods did not require their permission."


The Age of Grandeur is a Orthosian term for a time period across Anno Novis and Anno Halsyon, during which Orthosian civilizations generally reached their peak. Year 0 for the AOG has been determined by the first ever wish of a wizard on the continent, marking the self-reliance of mortal magics.
