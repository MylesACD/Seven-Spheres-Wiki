Purgatus

Universal Trait: use 3d6 instead of d20, when around very powerful devils no rolls flat 10.


Culture

Devils have vastly different amounts of hard and societal power. Hard-power comes from a form of social contract between all devils that establishes who is the most powerful amongst them. Initially all devils had the exact same amount of hard-power, individuals chose to centralize it by giving away their power, this period of the centralization and development was Anno Innectis. The societal structure is not nessacarily organized along the same lines as hard-power.
