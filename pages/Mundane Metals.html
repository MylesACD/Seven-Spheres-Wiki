Platinum

While platinum is much rarer than gold naturally and harder to mine its comparitive value to gold is brought down by its high mutability which allows for artificial supply.

Tin TODO

Tin is a very low value metal because of its negative magical conductivity, which makes is very difficult to place enchantments upon.
Along with its abundance and easy of use, this places tin in a prime position to serve as an everyday material for simple people.

Zinc

Regular zinc is very resistant to transmutation magic and echanting/threading in general. Anodized zinc is one of the most remarkable materials, having the lowest enchantability and lowest mutability of any mundane substance, coupled with very low fluxicity and a relatively low capacity for a metal. Due to these properties zinc has been nicknamed "dead/inert" metal and consequently was chosen as the material to be used for authentication tokens as agreed upon by DE and SH.
