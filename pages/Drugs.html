Drugs

Drugs can either be magical or mundane, with the magical ones varying greatly in effect, being more expensive, and being much more potent. Drugs, whether magical or mundane also fall into one of three classifications: natural, synthetic, or augmented. Augmented are synthetically enchanced versions of natural ones.

Frond: mundane, natural
Puff: mundane, varies
Mage Glass: magical, synthetic
