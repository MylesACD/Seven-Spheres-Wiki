Dark Pearl

A bead of darkness, the pure essence of the Underworld. The Dark Pearl also functions as a mobile, permanent, two-way portal to the Underworld. The pearl itself is encased in a small icosahedron of Uult's Metal, within a sphere of solid Adamantium. These protections are in two halves, bound together by magical locks in Fey, Infernal, Jot, and Primordial. The orb has “Dark Pearl, destroyer of planes, unending hunger of the night” inscribed in each of languages as well.
